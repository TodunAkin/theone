My work are explorations of ideas, concepts and designs.

I juxtapose different elements and find the balance between

them all.

Todun Akinyelure is an 20 year old designer and artist born

and raised in Lagos, Nigeria. He studied at Ridley college,

where he built his foundations in the IB visual arts SL classes.

During the Covid lockdown in Janaury 2021, He capitalized on

this time to develop himself and focus on evolving into a more

determined and driven creative. His vision is to inspire and help

people achieve their dreams and become better versions of

themselves.
